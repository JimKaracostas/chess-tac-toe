How to Play Chess-Tac-Toe

Goal: Get three of your pieces in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).

Playing the Game

  • Place: Select a piece above, then click an empty square
  • Move: Click your piece on the board, then click where to move it
  • Capture: Move to a square with an opponent's piece to capture it

Chess Movement Rules:

  • King (‚ôî/‚ôö): One square in any direction
  • Queen (‚ôï/‚ôõ): Any distance straight or diagonal
  • Rook (‚ôñ/‚ôú): Any distance horizontally or vertically
  • Bishop (‚ôó/‚ôù): Any distance diagonally
  • Knight (‚ôò/‚ôû): L-shape (2+1 squares), can jump over pieces
  • Pawn (‚ôô/‚ôü): One square forward, diagonal to capture

Hints:

  • Green squares: Valid moves
  • Red squares: Valid captures
  • Dark squares: Currently selected piece
